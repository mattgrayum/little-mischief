The Story

Get ready for Little Mischief—a soul-stirring, funk-fueled powerhouse from sunny California! This explosive fusion of rock, soul, funk, and Americana is fronted by the dynamic Michael Baker Jr. (Vocals/Guitar/Songwriter) and backed by the magnetic Zach Frese (Bass/Vocals), the pulsating Ben Parker (Drums), the rhythmic Matthew Grayum (Percussion), and the groove-machine Sam Kulchin (Guitar and Vocals) bringing the heat.


Little Mischief isn't just about music; they're a sonic explosion, igniting dance floors and transforming every beat into a legendary experience. Their chemistry, honed over countless nights of campfire jams and smoky studio sessions, delivers a sound that's as infectious as it is soulful. Expect to jump, dance, and even headbang as they weave their diverse setlist into an unforgettable experience.


When Little Mischief takes the stage, their music isn't just something you hear — it's something you live and breathe. Dive into the groove and experience a Little Mischief.
